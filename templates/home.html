What is Code Angels?

Code Angels is a platform for connecting software developers with non-profit organisations. Our goal is to help organisations do their work more efficiently without having to pay for the software. As a developer you get the chance to help make the world around you a better place.

How can I use the platform?

Non-profit Organisations: You can submit requests and we will do our best to match you with one (or more) of our angels who will develop the software you requested. Software Developers: First you need to create a profile. Next you can browse all active requests and apply for any of them. Alternatively we can send you projects based on your interests every other week and let you choose.
